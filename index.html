bFM on Spotify

bFM does a great job at playing great music. I can't listen to bFM 24/7, so I made a computer/programming thingy that goes to the bFM page, get all the songs from the playlists, searches spotify for the song, then for each DJ/Show makes a playlist of the most recent 50 or so songs.

This is that computer/programming thingy.

Important Notes

these playlists are neither 100% accurate nor 100% complete.

Many artists — especially some of the awesome NZ ones — are not on spotify.

Also, the computer gets it wrong sometimes, REALLY WRONG, no, the DJ didn't play Body Count by Justin Timberlake rather the self titled track... bear that in mind. Zac & Ethan's playlist is really really broken ( no they didn't play that track! ), I'll fix it soon.

Finally, this is not authorized or endorsed by bFM, I'm not sure what they'll think of it this stage. Comments or suggestions — @eadz
