bFM on Spotify

So I love music, but after a while, I need new music. Good is only good for the first 30 plays. bFM does a great job at playing great music. I made a computer thingy that goes to the bFM page, get all the songs from the playlists, searches spotify for the song, then for each DJ/Show makes a playlist of the most recent 30 or so songs.

This is that thingy.
